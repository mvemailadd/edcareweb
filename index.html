About Us

The Founder declares Edcare a.k.a. Education Care Rehabilitation Organization, a Charitable Trust working in the areas of Education, Care and Rehabilitation is irrevocable, non-political and non-religious in character.

About the Founder

The Founder, Prof. R. Amjad Hasan, M.A., PGDG&C, is a War Veteran assessed Exceptional at Work and Exemplary in Character by the Indian Air Force, and a Member at the American Psychological Association, is experienced at serving the Governmental Institutions at the Center and the state, and the Non-Governmental Organizations, beside the Professional colleges, schools and having been associated with serving at the Police and Law bodies in the State, has an accomplished experience from Warfare to Welfare, since 1987, and has been serving the society in the name of Edcare, a Trademark registered in 2004 with the Intellectual Properties Office at Chennai, has now resolved to commit his work to Charitable Trust to help the needy, free from discrimination whatsoever, in the areas of Education, Care and rehabilitation, and thus had made it possible, by its constitution.

  • "Education" encompasses a broad spectrum of activities aimed at fostering general awareness, training, coaching, mentoring, and preparation. It includes efforts to ensure, enable, improve, provide, facilitate, offer, and secure various objectives related to parenting, as well as the development of physical, behavioral, social, moral, and intellectual talents, skills, interests, aptitudes, and abilities
  • "Care" involves safeguarding and promoting human, environmental, and societal well-being. It encompasses various supportive activities aimed at establishing, enabling, and improving overall welfare, extending from prenatal care to economic prosperity. This includes ensuring rights, adhering to responsibilities, and accessing provisions outlined in constitutional and governmental frameworks, addressing various challenges individuals may face..
  • "Rehabilitation" means aiding those affected by social issues like addiction, abuse, poverty, and homelessness, providing support for education and care. It includes various measures such as training, counseling, and securing basic necessities, aiming for growth and development amid challenges like natural disasters or government declarations.
